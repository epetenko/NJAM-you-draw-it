Christie approval ratings

Draw your answer above.

Christie began with average approval ratings, which rose even higher in the aftermath of Hurricane Sandy. But things took a dramatic turn when the governor was implicated in the closure of the George Washington Bridge in late 2013 to 2014. Saladin Ambar, a senior scholar at the Center on the American Governor at Rutgers University, said his poll numbers reflect his poor handling of the controversy.

"Had he come out ahead of the scandal and accepted culpability, it may have taken out the sting a bit," he said.
